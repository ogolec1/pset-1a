Busch Gardens Williamsburg

in James City County, Virginia

Basic Information

Busch Gardens Williamsburg is a huge theme park owned by SeaWorld Entertainment is located in James City County, Virginia. Busch Gardens is a Europe-themed park with rides, coasters, shows, dining & river & rail attractions. The unique backdrop and breathtaking landscaping have earned the park the award of America’s Most Beautiful Theme Park. The park's rollercoasters are so exciting and thrilling.
